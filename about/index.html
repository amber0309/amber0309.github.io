Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. CHAN, Lai-Wan. Previously, I received the B. Eng degree from Department of Electrical Engineering in Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015, under the supervision of Prof. Feng Donghan.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in causal discovery and causality-based learning.
